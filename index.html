About Yvonne

A career devoted to children's
neurological wellbeing.

With over 30 years of clinical experience, Yvonne Luiz has built a practice founded on deep expertise, warm compassionate care, and an unwavering commitment to the children and families she serves.

After graduating with her BSc in Physiotherapy from the University of the Witwatersrand in 1992, she developed foundational skills across ICU and general ward settings before channelling her passion into paediatric neurology — the field that has defined her career.

Over the last 15 years, her practice has evolved into a fully dedicated Paediatric Neurology physiotherapy practice in Boksburg, providing specialised assessment and treatment for children with a wide range of neurological and developmental challenges.

Yvonne has also mentored 11 physiotherapists over the years, many of whom have gone on to lead departments and establish their own practices in South Africa, Canada, and New Zealand.

What We Offer

Specialist treatment
for every child.

Each child is unique. Treatment plans are individualised, evidence-based, and built in close collaboration with families and the broader medical team.

🧠
Paediatric Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialist treatment for children with cerebral palsy, genetic disorders, and neurological conditions using the Bobath NDT approach.

👶
Infant & Newborn Assessment

Early detection using GMA, HINE, and Newborn Behavioural Observations — certified through Harvard Medical School's Brazelton Institute.

🌀
Sensory Integration Therapy

Assessment and treatment of sensory processing difficulties affecting learning, behaviour, and daily functioning in children of all ages.

🚶
Gait Analysis & Orthotic Management

Advanced Elaine Owen Gait Analysis and orthotic management for children with neurological and orthopaedic walking difficulties.

🏫
School Screening Programmes

Weekly physiotherapy screening at local primary schools and preschools, identifying developmental concerns early for appropriate referral.

🤝
Community & Outreach

Providing physiotherapy services for children at a locally funded orphanage, and ongoing community screening across Boksburg.
